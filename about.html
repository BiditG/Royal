Our Story

Established in 2024, Royal Tinchuli Banquet has been a premier venue for memorable events in the heart of Kathmandu. Our mission is to provide an exceptional experience for our clients, offering exquisite service and a stunning setting for all occasions.

Our banquet hall combines elegance and versatility, accommodating weddings, corporate events, parties, and more. We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence and strive to make every event a unique and cherished experience.
